    I was in my second semistor of engineering when I visited Fablab for first time. I was glad after visiting because that was the environment I had wished to work in my school days. As Dhruv sir was student of Fablab I used to visit fablab frequently. One day at CEPT I was talking to sir about my hobby of playing flute, he suggested me to do a project of making an automatic flute playing machine. I have been working on this project since last 3 months. I searched on google if any machine playing flute automatically is made or not and came to know about various machines that were made to play flute automatically. After that I came to the conclution of upgrading the flute machine from complex to more simpler one.     Starting with the project

    This is the rough sketch I have made. Following are the components which I have used in my project:

    1. servo motor
    2. Arduino
    3. Blower Nozel
    4. Pressure Controller

    In this the servo motors will control the holes of flute and I heve taken Arduino Mega to control servo motor. The servo will react according to the tune given.

    The Blower Nozel and Pressure controller are yet to design.
